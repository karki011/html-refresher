About us

Created By: Subash Karki

Sitar Indian Cuisine, derives its name from a word “Sitar” which is a plucked stringed instrument famous with Indian classical music. What makes Sitar a famous musical instrument in Indian classical music is the harmonious resonance created while plucking its strings. Same thing applies to the Indian authentic taste. It’s the perfect blending of Indian spices used in Indian cuisines that creates harmonious buds pleasing taste.

Sitar Indian Cuisine is an authentic Indian restaurant in Birmingham offering North-Indian Dishes since 2001. Here, at Sitar we value our customers and believe that our customer’s satisfaction is our pleasure. We have thousands of satisfied customers who check in almost every day for buffet-lunch and A-La-Carte’. Besides buffet-lunch and dinner, we also provide indoor and outdoor catering services in Birmingham.We are conveniently located at south side downtown, near University Of Alabama at Birmingham (UAB).

Chicken tikka masala

Chicken Tikka Masala is chunks of chicken marinated in spices and yogurt, which is then broiled in a tandoor oven, and served in a masala sauce. The sauce usually includes tomatoes, frequently as puree; cream and/or coconut cream; and various spices. Have you ever tried recreating a restaurant style Chicken tikka at home??

Chicken Biryani

Biryani is a rice-based dish (specially, aromatic Basmati rice) cooked with spices and chicken, mutton, fish, eggs or vegetables. Biryani is a traditional Indian dish, very popular South Asian cuisine and a key element of any occasions like weddings, parties and other traditional functions.

Vegetable korma

Vegetable Korma is a creamy, mildly spiced, and extremely flavorful curry, which is popular in India as well as in central Asia. The curry base can be coconut paste , yogurt, cream, onion or tomato paste or a combination, depending upon whether it carries Mughlai or South Indian taste.
